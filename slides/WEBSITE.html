I am here.

web design

This project is a website with a small narrative. The structure is inspired by "Powers of Ten," a short film by Charles and Ray Eames. I went with a comic book feel. The left panels define who I is or the subject, and the right panels define here or the place. Everything begins large and general and gets more specific and zoomed in through the progression of the story.
