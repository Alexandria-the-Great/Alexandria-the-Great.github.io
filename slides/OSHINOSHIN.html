Oshin Oshin

band branding

OSHIN OSHIN is a two-piece rock band consisting of visual designer, lyric writer, singer, and guitar player, Alexandria Henry and composer and guitar player, Manuel Vargas. Their first album, golden repair is about the struggle of life and overcoming obstacles you found you yourself created, in order to find happiness. It is about recovering and being made stronger than before, so that you can live out your dreams in peace. Golden repair or kintsukuroi is the Japanese practice of and philosophy behind repairing broken pottery, using gold. The past is not hidden, it is highlighted. Repairs make you more valuable. At this time, no music is written, Manuel has begun work on ideas for songs, while Alexandria still has to learn guitar. She has created a visual identity for the band to step into whenever it is ready to really come alive.
