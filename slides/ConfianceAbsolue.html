Confiance Absolue

pixelation animation

This short film is a collaboration between me and classmate, Zoe Graham. Confiance absolue is French for absolute confidence. We had to have absolute trust in each other without hardly knowing the other. She trusted my ideas - to let me put dirt on her arm as it fell numb, to touch an egg that had been in my mouth, and to shoot and animate when she was not able. I trusted her to shoot while I lay on the table and she animated my hair.
