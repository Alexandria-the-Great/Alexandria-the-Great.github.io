Alexandria Henry

communication designer

My work spans many platforms. I aim to create multilayered pieces of work both conceptually and visually. I enjoy going back and forth and bringing the analogue into digital and digital into analogue while using a wide variety of techniques. My inspirations come from the beauty I find in the world around me. Each project is different and calls for a different treatment. Finding the visual language to express ideas most effectively is what I do best, besides baking delicious cakes!
